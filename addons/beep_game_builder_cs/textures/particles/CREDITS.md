## Notes

- These are **particle sprites**, not tiling textures. They do **not** suit
  `WeatherSystemComponent.CloudTexture`, which is scrolled and needs a seamless tile —
  a single puff would show an obvious seam. Author a tiling sheet for that.
- The pack has no **leaf** texture, so the `LeafFall` weather type still has no sprite.
- `.import` files are generated by Godot on first scan; they are not hand-written here.
